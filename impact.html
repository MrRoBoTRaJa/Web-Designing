Report Themes

Key objectives now presented as impact pillars.

Instead of raw annual-report wording, these blocks explain the trust’s priorities in a stronger website format.

Student Welfare

Educational support for poor and needy learners

The report explicitly mentions scholarships, educational funds, and wider institutional support for learning opportunities.

Awareness

Health and public information campaigns

Work includes awareness on HIV/AIDS, Hepatitis B, Pulse Polio, and health and eye-check programs across districts of Jharkhand.

Livelihood

Technical, vocational, and employment-linked training

The trust’s objectives include technical education, vocational provider support, employment networking, and skill development outreach.

Rural Development

Programs related to agriculture, irrigation, seed varieties, farmers training, and broader rural upliftment show the trust’s rural-development orientation.

Social Justice

The report also speaks about equality, anti-corruption, anti-dowry, anti-trafficking, social welfare, and tribal and backward community empowerment.

About The Trust

A community-rooted trust working for lasting social change.

The annual report describes the trust as being founded by social leaders, educationalists, and community leaders to support sustainable development in slums, rural communities, and among helpless women and children in Jharkhand.

Jharkhand-based Registered in 2010 People-centered
Who We Are

The trust works mainly in the urban and rural areas of Jharkhand state. Its purpose is not only relief, but also empowerment through socio-economic development, education, health awareness, social justice, and practical support programs.

The language in the report shows a broad grassroots mission: support for poor students, awareness initiatives, technical training, community development, women-focused self-reliance, tribal welfare, and environmental protection.

This page gives those ideas a cleaner public voice so the organization can present itself clearly to donors, collaborators, institutions, and the wider public.

Trust Identity

Official name: S.N.M. Memorial Educational Foundation Trust

Registration reference: Report notes registration dated 14.08.2010 under the Trust registration framework.

Main location: Ranchi, Jharkhand

Focus: Education, disability welfare, women empowerment, health awareness, skill development, environment, and community upliftment.

Mission And Vision

Practical development with dignity at the center.

The original report content is broad and mission-heavy. Here it is reorganized into clearer public-facing statements.

Mission

To create access to education, awareness, training, social justice, and welfare support for vulnerable communities, especially women, children, tribal groups, rural families, and underserved learners.

Vision

To help build stronger and more self-reliant communities in Jharkhand through inclusive development, public awareness, and opportunities that lead to long-term social upliftment.

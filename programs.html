Programs

Focused work across education, welfare, health, skills, and community life.

The annual report lists many objectives. This page organizes them into program areas visitors can actually understand and explore.

Education & Scholarships

Support for schools, play schools, nursery to higher education, scholarships for poor and needy students, law and technical education, libraries, night schools, and adult education efforts.

Computer Literacy & Technical Training

The report specifically mentions typing, computer training, technical education, and practical employment-linked training support.

Health Awareness & Camps

Awareness programs on HIV/AIDS, Hepatitis B, Pulse Polio, health awareness, eye check-up camps, and community health outreach.

Disability Welfare Support

Welfare work around awareness, diagnosis, training, rehabilitation support, and aids and appliances for physically handicapped individuals.

Women Livelihood & Security

Training in sewing, knitting, embroidery, handicraft, and broader work related to women security, dignity, social equality, and anti-trafficking awareness.

Environment & Plantation

Programs on environment protection, plantation, pollution control, and public awareness related to natural resources and sustainability.

Agriculture & Rural Development

Sustainable agriculture, irrigation facilities, farmer training, seed use, medicinal plants, horticulture, sericulture, and floriculture-related development support.

Skill Development & Placement Readiness

Vocational training providers in uncovered rural and backward areas, employment support, and awareness on skill development for school dropouts and disadvantaged groups.

Health Outreach

Taking awareness, care, and preventive support closer to communities.

The trust's health work includes awareness on HIV/AIDS, Hepatitis B, Pulse Polio, eye checkup camps, community health education, and outreach for vulnerable groups who need timely information and support.

What We Do

Awareness and outreach that can change daily life.

This page gives the trust's health outreach work a dedicated identity instead of leaving it as a small mention on the homepage.

Health Awareness Campaigns

Programs that spread practical knowledge about public health, prevention, and safe community practices.

Eye Checkup Camps

Support for camps and field-based outreach that bring basic eye health attention closer to people.

Preventive Health Information

Community messaging focused on awareness, early response, and public health preparedness.

Support for Vulnerable Groups

Health-linked programs for communities that may otherwise have limited access to information or timely care.

Why It Matters

Health outreach can prevent suffering before it becomes more serious. Camps, awareness drives, and trusted local communication help families make safer and more informed decisions.

By creating a separate page for health outreach, your website now shows this work as a major trust priority instead of a single short line in the homepage card.
